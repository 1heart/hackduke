Check Out What We Have Planned!

Check-In

Check-in will be from 12:00 PM - 2:15 PM in front of the Fitzpatrick Center (map). If you are a Duke student, please make sure you come early because there is a set number of reserved spots and we have no idea how quickly they will fill up.

Parking

There's a football game on Saturday, so parking is a little bit tricky. Please read carefully, and plan accordingly:

  • Parking is located in the Bryan Center Parking Garage (PG4)
  • If you're arriving between 11:30 AM and 3:30 PM Saturday, then you must enter campus via Erwin road onto Research drive. At this point you will receive a parking pass to proceed onto PG4
  • Parking passes will be $5.00 for the weekend (Negotiated down from $7/hr!)

Rules

  • Keep your name tags on. You will not be allowed on the premises without it.
  • Teams of 4 please!
  • Keep your valuables close to you. We are not responsible for lost items.
  • Absolutely no alcohol and drugs!
  • No working in advance.
  • In case of emergency, call Duke police (919) 684-2444) (life-threatening, 911) AND inform one of us
  • If you need assistance, look for people in royal blue color shirts.
  • Don't lose your meal tickets! (Duke students: your friends who are not participating cannot get food)
  • Be respectful of each other.
  • If you engage in any activity that threatens or damages the infrastructure of Duke University, including but not limited to damage to property, vandalism, and (actual) hacking into the Duke network, or the lives of any participant or staff, you will not be allowed back.
  • Check your email/Piazza! Important notifications and announcements will be sent out to everyone via email.
  • Have fun!

Frequently Asked Questions

When? Where?

Starts: November 16, 2pm
Ends: November 17, 5pm
Hosted on Duke University's West Campus

What can I make?

We welcome all software and hardware hacks.
We will have lab space for hardware hackers.

Who can I work with?

Anyone! Get to know your fellow programmers better from your own school and from other schools as well!
The only request that we have is that you have a max of 4 people on your team and that you make a new friend.

Travel?

If you're from coming in from UMD, UVA, VTech, GATech, or in-state we'll be sending you a bus or reimbursing travel costs. Reach out to us (hackers@hackduke.org) if you haven't already secured yourself a seat on the bus. But hurry! Spots are running out!

Did someone say prizes?

And a bunch of other prizes :D

And more to be announced soon!
